Ascalon

All the cultures of the world once revered the dragon (in one form or another) — but, just like in our world, people today sneer at the unscientific.

A grainy image of a dragon is leaked on the nascent internet, and Selene, the scientist, finds herself on its trail at the peak of the highest mountain. She will contend with encountering the divine, a traitorous crew-mate and tyrannical captain, and, finally, overcoming the guilt of what she did to get on the trip in the first place…
